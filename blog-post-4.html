Basic Commands Every Dog Should Know

Posted on September 8, 2025 by The TinDog Team

Training is an essential part of a dog's life. It strengthens the bond between you and your pup, provides mental stimulation, and ensures their safety. Mastering these basic commands is the foundation of good behavior.

1. Sit

This is often the first command a dog learns. It's a simple way to manage excited behavior and is a great starting point for more complex training.

2. Stay

The "stay" command is a crucial safety skill. It can prevent your dog from running into a dangerous situation, like a busy street. Patience and positive reinforcement are key to teaching this command effectively.
