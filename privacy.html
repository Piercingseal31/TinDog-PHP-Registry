By creating an account and using our Service, you explicitly consent to the collection, processing, and use of your personal information as described in this Policy.

We process your personal information for legitimate purposes, including to operate and maintain the TinDog service, to communicate with you, to improve our platform, and to ensure the safety and security of our users.

5. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you are entitled to the following rights:

  • The Right to be Informed: You have the right to know how and why we process your data.
  • The Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data.
  • The Right to Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • The Right to Rectification: You can correct any inaccurate data we have on file.
  • The Right to Erasure or Blocking: You have the right to suspend, withdraw, or order the removal of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@tindog.net.

6. Data Security and Retention

We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. We will retain your data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.
