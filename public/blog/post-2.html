The Best Toys for Active Pups

Posted on September 12, 2025 by The TinDog Team

Keeping an active dog entertained is crucial for their physical and mental health. The right toys can prevent boredom, reduce anxiety, and provide a great outlet for their energy. Here are our top picks for toys that will keep your energetic companion engaged.

1. Interactive Puzzle Toys

Puzzle toys challenge your dog's mind. They typically require the dog to solve a problem—like sliding a piece or lifting a flap—to get a treat. This mental stimulation is just as tiring as physical exercise.

2. Durable Chew Toys

For powerful chewers, durability is key. Look for toys made from strong, non-toxic rubber. These are great for dental health and can withstand hours of chewing, saving your furniture from a similar fate.
