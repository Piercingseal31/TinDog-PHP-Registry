General

TinDog is a social application designed to help dog owners find and connect with other dog owners in their area for playdates, walks, and friendships. It's a fun and easy way to help your dog socialize!

Profile & Matching

It's simple! In the "Find Matches" section, you swipe right on a dog's profile if you'd like your pup to meet them, and left if not. If the other dog owner also swipes right on your dog's profile, it's a match! You can then find them in "My Matches" and start chatting.

Navigate to the "My Profile" page from the sidebar. From there, you will see an "Edit Profile" button. On the edit screen, you can switch between "Dog's Information" and "Owner's Information" using the tabs at the top to update your details.

In the "Settings" page, you can adjust the "Maximum Distance" and "Age Range" sliders. This will filter the dogs you see in the "Find Matches" section to better suit your preferences for location and playmate age.
