Top 5 Dog-Friendly Parks in Cebu

Posted on September 10, 2025 by The TinDog Team

Cebu is a fantastic place for dog owners, with plenty of green spaces to explore. If you're looking for the perfect spot for your next walk or playdate, we've sniffed out the best dog-friendly parks in the area.

1. Ayala Center Cebu Family Park

A well-known spot, this park offers wide-open spaces perfect for a game of fetch. It's a great place for dogs to socialize while their humans relax.

2. SM Seaside Central Park

Located by the sea, this park provides a refreshing breeze and a beautiful view. The pathways are perfect for a leisurely stroll with your furry friend.
